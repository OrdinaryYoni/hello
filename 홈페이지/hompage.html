For Change: PWR Leading the Water Purification Revolution

PWR is dedicated to preventing the improper disposal of liquids that could contaminate groundwater.By safeguarding the environment and promoting eco-friendly solutions, PWR aims to ensure a secure water future for Human.

Collaboration of microorganisms and sand, ‘Bio Sand Filter’ for clean drinking water

There are still one billion people in the world who do not have access to clean drinking water. For this purpose, PWR developed a Bio Sand Filter to purify water. This filter uses 'slow filtration' technology to purify water, allowing people to drink clean water.

PWR launches industrial wastewater purification device

This year, on March 4, 2023, PWR launched an industrial wastewater purification device. Lee Ga-yeon, director of the development team, confidently responded, “This device will purify all wastewater generated at indusrties in future very cleanly.”

Building wells in water-scarce countries PWR CEO Lee Sol-hwa

Starting with sponsoring a drinking water pump in Krachi West, Ghana, CEO LEE has donated over 300 million won to a total of 26 drinking water facilities and one drinking water project in 10 countries including Malawi, Zambia, Bangladesh, and Cambodia over the past 12 years, becoming a major sponsor of World Vision in 2018. He was appointed as the 35th member of the Bob Pierce Honor Club.
